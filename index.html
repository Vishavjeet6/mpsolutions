Services

Custom Survey Design

We work closely with healthcare companies and web designers to create surveys that align with your objectives and user demographics. Whether you need HCP or Patient satisfaction surveys, health risk assessments, or feedback forms, we tailor the design to capture relevant information effectively with end-users in mind.

Responsive Survey Development

With the increasing prevalence of mobile devices in healthcare, we prioritize responsive survey development. Our surveys are optimized for various screen sizes and devices, ensuring a seamless user experience across desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

Data Security and Compliance

We adhere to the highest standards of data security and compliance to safeguard sensitive healthcare information. Our survey programming services comply with industry standards, ensuring the confidentiality and integrity of patient data.

Usability Testing

Conduct usability testing to ensure that the survey interface is intuitive and user-friendly for targeted audiences such as patients, caregivers, and healthcare professionals. Test various aspects of the survey such as navigation, question clarity, and response options to identify potential usability issues and improve the overall user experience to minimise data fatigue.

Overlays-Multilingual Surveys

In multicultural healthcare settings, language barriers can hinder effective communication. Our multilingual survey capabilities enable you to reach diverse patient populations and gather feedback in multiple languages, fostering inclusivity and accessibility. Our team will overlay local languages and local link testing to meet your expectations.

Cross-device Compatibility

In addition, our team test surveys across different devices paying attention on screen sizes to ensure responsiveness and functionality of the device used be it a desktop, laptop, tablet, or smartphones. Importantly, we seek to verify that the survey display correctly and function properly on various operating systems and all web browsers.
